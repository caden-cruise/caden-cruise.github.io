The Story of Dark Souls Remastered

Prologue

"In the Age of Ancients, the world was unformed, shrouded by fog. A land of gray crags, Archtrees and Everlasting Dragons. But then there was fire, and with fire came disparity. Heat and cold, life and death, and of course, light and dark. Then from the dark, they came, and found the Souls of Lords within the flame. Nito, the first of the dead, the Witch of Izalith, and her Daughters of Chaos, Gwyn, the Lord of Sunlight, and his faithful knights, and the furtive pygmy, so easily forgotten."

"With the Strength of Lords, they challenged the Dragons. Gwyn's mighty bolts peeled apart their stone scales. The Witches weaved great firestorms. Nito unleashed a miasma of death and disease. And Seath the Scaleless betrayed his own, and the Dragons were no more."

"Thus began the Age of Fire. But soon, the flames will fade, and only Dark will remain. Even now, there are only embers, and man sees not light, but only endless nights. And amongst the living are seen, carriers of the accursed Darksign."

"Yes, indeed. The Darksign brands the Undead. And in this land, the Undead are corralled and led to the north, where they are locked away, to await the end of the world.
... This is your fate."

-Dark Souls, opening cinematic


Locations

Lordran
The land of ancient Lords. This is the location of where the game takes place. This place is filled with many creatures, and this is where the First Flame waits to be linked.
Anor Londo
The city of the gods. Located on top of a mountain, the cliff face and its own walls have separated it from the outside world. Invitations were once given to great champions, but this practice has been forgotten over time. Sen's Fortress sists below, an enormous death trap. The truth is hidden behind an illusion.
Lost Izadtth
Legendary city of the Witch of Izadtth. This city is founded deep underground. The Witch and her daughters practiced a form of fire sorcery known as pyromancy. The art was lost when the city was destroyed by the Flame of Chaos.
The Catacombs
Extensive network of tombs and crypts that are overseen by the Gravelord Nito. All things that die are laid to rest here. The area is not peaceful, occultists attempted to steal power and use it against the gods. The items used can still be found in the tunnels.
New Londo
Large city of the Undead, a once prosperous and known for the foresight of its leaders. The undead were welcome and it became a safehaven. This city was teeming with life. This city was later flooded, killing all inside. Leaving the dead still wandering as ghosts through the ruins.
Oolacile
Ancient and a long-forgotten kingdom of the humans that was once in Lordan. A land of scholars, who practiced a lost form of light-based sorcery. The sorcerers were corrupted, and the city kingdom was lost.
Astora
Another human kingdom, known for its knights, clergy, and nobidtty. The origin of the Way of White resides here.
Catarina
A kingdom outside Lordran that is known for its drinks and festivities. It is also known for its peculiar armor. Though highly effective it is still mocked by outsiders.
The Great Swamp
A distant and inhospitable land outside Lordran. Unwanted by all, but those who call this place home. Populated by outcasts and exiles. All share a common interest in the teachings of pyromancy and shamanism.
Vinheim
Known for the Dragon School, a system of study formalized by the Seath the Scaleless. The school employs its own spies and assassins. Through this they maintain control of the city.
Carim
The kingdom is known for its shady knights, aristocrats, and gods. It is a place known for its distinctive weaponry.
Undead Asylum
Asylum for the undead at the edge of Lordran. They were corralled here, and many other places. They were kept in hopes that one day one of them would be chose and able to defeat the gatekeeper.

Characters

Gwyn, The Lord of Sunlight
Strongest Lord Soul, Gwyn's power takes the form of lightning. He was the founder of Anor Londo and linked the First Flame, doing so became a Lord of Cinder. He is referred to as a god, either by himself or a title given by others. He is the father of The Firstborn, Gwynevere, and Gwyndolin.
The Nameless Firstborn
The firstborn son of Gwyn.
Gwynevere, Princess of Sunlight
The second daughter of Gwyn. She left Anor Londo when the First Flame began to fade. Gwyndolin created an illusion of her to maintain the image of power and stability in the kingdom. This illusion grants the Lordvessel to successful Undead.
Gwyndolin, The Dark Sun
The youngest son of Gwyn, and the only god in Anor Londo. He has a deep adoration for the sun, and spent time guarding Gwyn's tomb and attempting to delay the Age of Dark. He was raised as a daughter due to his magic skills and connection to the moon. He specializes in sourcery using Faith instead of Intelligence. With the ability to use powerful illusion spells that are undistinguishable from reality, he makes Anor Londo appear to be thriving. Although the city had been abandoned long ago.
Kingseeker Frampt
Primordial Serpent, seeks to find Undead with the potential to be a Lord, and directs them to link the First Flame. In doing so would prolong the Age of Darkness. Frampt denouces the player if they ever join Darksalker Kaathe instead.
Dragonslayer Ornstein
The captian of Gwyn's four greatest knights. He was granted a fragment of Gwyn's soul for his service in the war against the dragons. He guards the illusions of Gwynevere, testing the strength for the Undead looking to link the First Flame. His feats with the dragons were said to be legendary, and he has been said to have split a bolder. He also wields a spear infused with lightning to penetrate dragon scales.
Hawkeye Gough
A knight of Gwyn, known for using massive greatbows to shoot down dragons. Gough could fell a dragon with a single arrow larger than a tree. Since the war ended, Gough simply spends his time binding spoken messages into carved archtree wood.
Lord's Blade Ciaran
One of Gwyn's assasians, and also one of the four knights. Out of respect for Artorias she treats humans fairly even though she is prejudiced against them.
Artorias the Abysswalker
Legendary knight of Gwyn, rumored to have forged a pact with the beasts of the Abyss to fight the Darkwraiths, but this is a fabrication. He was defeated by the Abyss, and his efforts were continued by a forgotten champion. The champion defeated Manus and stalled the Abyss's growth. Due to Artorias being friends with Sif the Great Wolf and cat Alvina, both now guard his grave from anyone who attempts to enter the Abyss.
Great Grey Wolf Sif
A battle compainon of Artorias, he now guards the grave of Artorias and deters anyone from trying to endter the abyss for their own means.
Alvina
Ally of Artorias, she now leads a group of bandits to defend his grave. If approached from Darkroot Forest the covenant will attack. Players can join her covenant and defend the grave from intruders, adn will be allowed free access to it.
Velka, Goddess of Sin
A black-haird witch and rouge goddess. She keeps and recordes all of the sins committed by humans, these can be resolved, but for a price.
The Witch of Izalith
One of the four original Lord Soul bearers, her's grants the power of fire. She is a master of long forgotten fire sorceries. In attempt to recreate the First Flame, she created the Flame of Chaos. Marking the birth of demons. This flame transformed her and her daughters into demos barring Quelana, who escaped. The creation of Chaos also created pyromancy as a byproduct.
Quelana
A daughter of Izalith, and the only successful individual to escape the destruction of her home. She adapted many old fire sorcery spells into pyromancy, and taught them to Salaman. Salaman in turn taught it to the outcases who live in the Great Swamp. She hoped to teach people control over the flame to prevent another disaster.
Quelaag
A daughter of Chaos, who fled Izalith but was unable to escape before being transformed by the Flame of Chaos. She is a demon and resides near one of the two Bells of Awakening with her sister, The Fair Lady.
The Fair Lady
A daughter of Chaos and also a firekeeper. She is afflicted by stillborn eggs that serve as a cradle for tiny Humanity sprites. She is tended to by her Chaos Servants. She maintains a bonfire and can assist an Undead by upgrading Estus Flasks. She can only be understood if they have the correct magic ring. Members of her covanent can trade humanities for powerful pyromancies, and access the city of Izalith through a secret entrance.
Gravelord Nito
One of the original Lord Soul bearers, Nito's soul grants power over death. He can inflict disease and decay, as well as, he animates skeletons to fight for him. He played a major role in defeating the dragons, and resides over an extended system of crypts and tombs and watches over the dead. He leads the covanent, Gravelord Servants, who use Eyes of Death to spread curses. Though more recently the necromancer Pinwheel has been slowly draining Nito's power for his own use.
Pinwheel
A necromancer of the Catacombs, who has been taking power from Nito's soul for his own ends. He appears to have three heads and six arms. He is not especially pwerful, but his three masks are each enchanted with special power, and fortifies the body of anyone wearing them.
Seath the Scaleless
Born without the scales that make other dragons immortal. He betrayed them and stole the primordial crystal, which granted him near complete invulnerability, if not an adequate imitation of it. He was given the title Duke by Gwyn, and a fragment of Gwyn's soul. He is known as the father of sorcery by the Dragon School of Vinheim. He tried to find the secrets of immortality. His research ended up driving him mad. He developed sorceries based on a link between souls and crystals. His tail can be severed to obtain the Moonlight Greatsword.
Big Hat Logan
Famous sorcerer who studied ath the Vinheim Dragon School of sorcery. He created powerful sorceries on par with Lord Gwyn's lightning. He would eventually go mad when he recreates Seath's crystal breath as a spell.
The Furtive Pygmy
One of the original four Lords that waged war on the dragon. Along with Gwyn, Nito, and the Witch of Izalith. The records and contributions of the pygmy were stricken from history records due to Gwyn fearing the Dark Soul the pygmy possessed.
The Four Kings
Rulers of New Londo, city of Undead. They were granted fragments of Gwyn's Lord Soul as recognition for their leadership. They peacefully ruled New Londo for a time. They encountered Darksalker Kaathe, who taught them Lifedrain. Their knights became Darkwraiths after joining him. They now reside in the Abyss, and are trapped in New Londo which was flooded to contain them.
Darkstalker Kaathe
A primordial serpent, who acts to end the Age of Fire and begin the Age of Dark. It is believed he tricked the people of Oolacile to revive Manus. He argues the player should forsake the First Flame and become a Lord of Dark. The Darkwraiths now answer to Kaathe. If the player side with Kaathe, Frampt will denounce them and leave. If the player sides with Frampt, Kaathe will never show himself.
Manus, Father of the Abyss
A primeval human, who became a twisted abomination after being resurrected. His Humanity goes wild, and mutates any other human it comes in contact with. He is a dark sorcerer of immense power, he wields the strength of the Abyss to bring the kingom of Oolacile to its knees and captured Princess Dusk. The Abyss spreads under his guidance and haults with his death. This is only a temporary measure. The knite Artorias attempts to stop him, but is defeated. Manus is warped beyon reason and seeks his pendant. He has snatched two people from the future and drug them back to the past.
Stone Dragon of Ash Lake
Desendant of the ancient dragons, and also the leader of a covenant that seek to become dragons themselves. The player can join this covenant to recieve the Dragon Greatsword. Which can be obtained by cutting of the dragon's tail. The dragon does not become hostile if the tail is cut off.
Black Dragon Kalameet
Ancient and powerful dragon, and a survivor of war against dragons. Gwyn and the other Lords dared not to provoke him, due to his superior strength. This can be a difficult fight for the player, and if Kalameet is killed the player will recieve a curesed ring. If his tail is severed the player recieves the Obsidian Greatsword.
Solaire of Astora
Knight Solaire is a Warrior of Sunlight covenant follower. He worships Gwyn and desires to find sun of his own. He is an ordinary human and his power comes from his training and dedication to his goal. He has two ends to his questline. If he finds the Sunlight Maggot, a parasitic demon, he will be driven insane. If the player kills the Maggot before he can find it, Solaire will recover from his dispair, and offer his assistance agaist Gwyn.
Andre of Astora
A blacksmith, trained in smithing of divine and mundane weapons. He can reinforce them to max strength or infuse them with divine power to gain bonus damage from faith.
Siegmyer of Catarina
Knight of Catarina, who came to Lordran when he became Undead. He searches for adventure and players can find him in a variety of predicaments throughout the game. His questline is tied to his daughter Sieglinde, and both must be completed.
Sieglinde of Catarina
Daughter of Siegmyer, she is searching for her father. If the questline is completed, she will find him, but see he has gone hollow. She decides to kll him over and over until he can rest permanently.
Laurentius of the Great Swamp
Pyromancer in the Great Swamp, who was overjoyed when he became Undead. He ventured to Lordran to meet Quelana, mother of pyromancy. He teaches basic pyromancy spells, and gives them part of his own pyromancy flmae if they dont have them. If the player finds Quelana, they can tell him and he will leave to search for her. But will eventually wander to Blighttown and goes hollow.
Eingyi
Servant of the Fair Lady, and guards her against intruders. He bears eggs as a sign of devotion to the Fair Lady's suffering. He was once a pyromancer from the Great Swamp, but was driven out when he created poison clouds from flame and inflicted prolonged suffering. He is a skilled pyromancer, and will share his knowledge with those who show as much devotion to the Fair Lady as he does.
Griggs of Vinheim
Admirer of Big Hat Logan, and hopes to study with him Grigg's attire suggests he could be a Vinheim spy, but his intentions are unknown.
Blacksmith Rickert of Vinheim
A former student of Vinheim, he is found in a cell near the upper edge of New Londo. He is there willingly and stays because it safe form hollows. He is a mage-smith and can imbue player's weapons with magic, allowing them to gain bonus damage from Intelligence.
Ingward, The Sealer
One of the three healers who devised the plan to flood New Londo to contain the Darkwraiths. The three swore to watch over the city but Ingward is the only that remaned. He knows sorceries that can cure curses. Which is a power said to be equal to divine acts. The corpse and spell of the second sealer is found in Blighttown, with their poison curing sorcery implies they were trying to cure the suffering there. The final sealer is found with Lautrec in Anor Londo, tho no longer wearing their mask which suggest they have forsaken their oath.
Princess Dusk of Oolacile
Princess of the human kingdom of Oolacile, she is trapped in one of Seath's crystal golems. She owes the player a debt of gratitude. She will teach the player sorceries of her kingdom. The player can summon her and learn long forgotten light-based sorceries. She is eventually captured by Manus, who pulls the player into the past when they try to investigate.
Patches the Hyena
A wandering merchant, travelling the world and selling his wears. He is known for tricking people and luring them into death traps, then looting their corpses for items to sell. If the player forgives him for this, he will eventually offer to sell items to the player. He holds a distain for clerics, and will never make peace if the player is one.
Crossbreed Priscilla
Half-dragon hybrid. She was a forlorn outcast, and feared for her power of lifehunt. Her weapons are imbued with the power of lifehunt, which causes heavy bleeding in living things. She asks the player to return to their world when first encountered. Her tail can be cut to find a special weapon.
The Gaping Dragon
Desendant from ancient dragons. Was consumed by gluttony and slowly transformed into a giant mouth. It must be killed to get the key to Blighttown. Cutting off its tail will give the Dragon King Greataxe.
Marvelous Chester
Mystery man snatched from the future by Manu and stranded in the past. He serves as a merchant.
Elizabeth
Giant Mushroom, the guardian to the sancutary in Oolacile and the godmother to Princess Dusk/
