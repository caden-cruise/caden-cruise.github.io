Character Build Designs

The builds displayed in this page are strictly for the players intending on playing PvE (Player versus Enviornment) or co-op play. They are designed for players wanting to play solo or co-op focused on winning boss battles and defeating regular enemies. Included will be builds created by other players. This is not an all inclusive list of all builds done in Dark Souls, but is a good starting point for new beginning players.

PvE Builds

Versatile PvE Build
The Versatile PvE build was created by DMCbr. Its main stats are Strength and Vitality, due to the nature of using large weapons and armor sets. This build focuses on being highly versatile due to its high values in defense, poise, attack and HP. It also has a variety of weapons that can be used for different situations. The build also includes healing, pyromancy, and other ranged spells. The armor and rings provide the overall best defense, stability, high poise, and HP and includes mid roll. The main weapon set is good for exploration and combat, and the boss set is good for dealing massive damage and allows for extra healing.
Build Equipment:

Right Hand: Quelaag's Furysword

Left Hand: Greatshield of Artorias

Head: Giant Helm

Chest: Giant Armor

Hands: Giant Gauntlets

Legs: Giant Leggings

Rings: Havel's Ring, Ring of Favor and Protection

Items: Firebomb, Black Firebomb, Throwing Knife, Estus Flask, Green Blossum

Demon Hunter
The Demon Hunter build was created by EarthScraper. The main purpose of this build is to do massive damage to bosses. This build however provides a challenge due to the nature that it has no poise or shield to block incoming damage.
Build Equipment:

Right Hand: Iaito

Left Hand: Pyromancy Flame, Parrying Dagger

Head: Wanderer's Hood

Chest: Chester's Coat

Hands: Leather Gloves

Legs: Black Leather Boots

Rings: Ring of Favor and Protection, Dark Wood Grain Ring

Spells: Sunlight Blade, Darkmoon Blade, Wrath of The Gods, Power Within

Power Within Rush
The Power Within Rush build was created Lemnear. The purpose of this build is to quickly overpower any of the bosses by massive damage output. This class is designed for multiple playthroughs and into NG++.
Build Equipment:

Right Hand: Strongest weapon depending on soul level and stats, to handle most enemies with 2 attacks, Ascended Pyromancy Flame

Left Hand: Glass Crest Shield, Basic Pyromancy Flame

Head: Crown of Dusk

Chest: Determined by Player

Hands: Determined by Player

Legs: Determined by Player

Rings: Havel's Ring, Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring

Spells: Power Within, Greater Fireball, Greater Combustion

Sage Freke
The Sage Freke build was created by tanner.jones. This build is designed to allow all players to use sorceries and wear medium armor. The downside to this build is its low damage output in the beginning and a verly low health pool.
Build Equipment:

Right Hand: Iato, Pyromancy Flame

Left Hand: Glass Crest Shield or Sanctus, Tin Crystallization Catalyst

Head: Crown of Dusk

Chest: Cantarina Armor

Hands: Cantarina Gauntlets

Legs: Cantarina Leggings

Rings: Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring, Lingering Dragoncrest Ring

Spells: Power Within, Greater Magic Weapon, Soul Spear

Dragon Hero
The Dragon Hero build was created by Kalibur. This build is designed to be a jack of all trades build. The main components are speed and range, but can quicly end fights given the opportunity. The downside is that it has some of a learning curve given it has very little armor, speed and dodging are the primary concept with this build.
Build Equipment:

Right Hand: Dragonslayer Spear, Greatsword of Artorias, Great Lord Greatsword

Left Hand: Glass Crest Shield or Sanctus, Oolacile Ivory Catalyst, Galder Shield, Pyromancy Flame

Head: Determined by Player

Chest: Determined by Player

Hands: Determined by Player

Legs: Determined by Player

Rings: Ring of Favor and Protect

Spells: Determined by Player

Artorias Build
The Artorias Build was created by FourmisYaha. The main purpose of this build is to strictly role-play as Knight Artorias (information about him can be found on the game lore page). There are some adaptations that the player can make, but this is strictly a role-playing build.
Build Equipment:

Right Hand: Abyss Greatsword, Greatsword of Artorias, Greatsword of Artorias (cursed)

Left Hand: Greatshield of Artorias, Clensing Sheild

Armor: Set of Artorias

Rings: Covenant of Artorias, Wolf Ring

Items: Silver Pendant

The Think Tank
The Think Tank build was created by Optimum. The purpose of this build is to strictly tank damage, have high HP, and intense defense.
Build Equipment:

Right Hand: Murakumo, Demon's Spear, Lifehunt Sythe, Dragon Tooth, Gravelord Sword, or Quelaag's Furysword, Ascended Pyromancy Flame

Left Hand: Greatshield of Artorias, Grass Crest Shield, Eagle Shield, Bloodshield, Black Bow of Pharis

Head: Black Iron Helm

Chest: Black Iron Armor

Hands: Black Iron Gauntlets

Legs: Black Iron Leggings

Rings: Ring of Favor and Protection, Cloranthy Ring/Ring of Steel Protection

Again this is not an all inclusive list but merely some examples of build ideas for new players to the game of Dark Souls.
Players can take the builds in this guide and adapt them to their needs, as well as, find other builds that may be better suited for what they would like to see in their own playthrough.
