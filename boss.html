Dark Souls Bosses

Bosses are powerful enemies that are placed throught the entire game of Dark Souls. They are determined by the names above their health bar, a larger amount of health (that shows at the bottom of the screen). Bosses can only be defeated in the player's world, and other players can assist in defeating bosses. In this guide the Bosses that will be covered will be the Area Bosses and the Expansion bosses that are pertinate to the story of the basegame and the DLC.

Area Bosses

Area Bosses are usually located at the end of eac area. They have a name above their health bar, located at the bottom of the screen. There are also fog-gates that block the area into the boss room.

Ornstein and Smough
Ornstein and Smough is a pair boss fight that depending on which is killed first determins the course of the fight. If Smough is defeated first, Ornstien will absorb Smough's power and double in size but retain his speed and lightning powers. If Ornstien is defeated first, Smough will kill Ornstien with his hammer and imbue it with Lightning power. Once both are defeated the player is awarded 50,000 souls. Depending on which is defeated last determins what boss soul is given to the player.

Weaknesses: Poision, Toxic, Bleed, Magic, Fire

Expansion Bosses

Expansion Bosses are Bosses that can be found in the Artorias of the Abyss DLC. These bosses are not necessary to complete the base game of Dark Souls, however, they are required to be defeated if a player looking to complete the entire story including the DLC.

Weaknesses: Standard Damage

The bosses included in this list are the main bosses that are required for progression through the story. Bosses that are not included are Mini-Bosses that can be found throughout the story, but are not optional to complete the base game or the DLC.
