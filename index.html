What is Dark Souls Remastered?

Dark Souls Remastered is a remastered version of the orignial Dark Souls released in 2011. The remastered version includes the base game and the Artorias of the Abyss DLC in high graphics and 60fps. Included in this site is information about the story of Dark Souls Remastered and information about bosses and different character builds that can be found in the navigation bar.

Gameplay

Description

The game is a 3rd person action roleplaying game, with a key mechanic being exploration. Dark Souls takes place in a large and continuous enviornment connected to a central hub area. A player can explore varios paths however, certain prerequisites must be met to unlock certain areas.

Bonfires

Key element of gameplay. These bonfires are scattered all throught the world and are level checkpoints. Resting at these a player's character is healed to full. They can also level up and perform other functions. However, resting here will respawn all the world enemies, excluding bosses, mini-bosses, and friendly non-playable characters (NPC's).

Combat

This game includes a great deal of combat. These include melee attacks, defensive manuvering, and magic abilities. For melee the player characters have access to high fantasy, medieval-style weapons. For defense the players have access to shields, armour, dodging, and parrying. For magic abilities, these include offensive and defensive abilities known as sorceries, miracles, and pyromancies. All these forms of combat have various pros and cons, as well as, in-game costs associated with them.

Multiplayer

Dark Souls also has a "humanity" system. This means there are two playable forms. A hollow form happening whenever a player dies in the game. This form does not allow the player to summon other players or non-playable characters (NPCs) to assist them. By consuming a humanity the player enters a human form. Allowing them to summon other players or non-playable characters (NPCs) to assist them. While in human form, this can allow other players to invade and kill the player.
